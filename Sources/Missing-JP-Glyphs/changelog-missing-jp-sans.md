# Changelog for Missing JP Sans

## 2025-09-22

*This update includes characters that are in the [Educational Basic list in the Glyphs app](https://github.com/3type/glyphs-han) (for which I consider urgently needed characters, and besides the original Simplified Chinese version, I also converted it to a Traditional Chinese version), characters with the 亻 radical that are found in commonly used Traditional Chinese character lists, and some miscellaneous characters. Most of the sources are now based off the v2.005 version of Source Han Sans.*
